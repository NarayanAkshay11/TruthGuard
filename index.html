A Novelle solution for fake news

Our project aims to develop a browser extension for assessing the credibility of news articles. Using a trained neural network (NN) model with around 83% accuracy, the extension scans web page text to predict the likelihood of information being true. It offers credibility indicators, like a color-coded score or label, to guide users. The extension is designed to work across popular browsers like Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. Our next steps include implementing the NN model, creating a user-friendly interface, thorough testing, user feedback, and ensuring compliance with security and privacy standards. The final product will be released through official browser extension stores, with the goal of helping users navigate news content more confidently.
